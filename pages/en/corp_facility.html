Facilities Management is an interdisciplinary field devoted to the coordination of space, infrastructure, people and organization for better production.

 

Production Engineering

is a combination of manufacturing technology with management science. A production engineer typically has a wide knowledge of engineering practices and is aware of the management challenges related to production. The goal is to accomplish the production process in the smoothest, most-judicious and most-economic way.

 

HIPPO Valve Production. Our engineers design, implement and refine products, services, processes and systems with fully integrated resources. Our team is required to consider physical, human and financial resources at high efficiency and low cost, yet considering the possibility of continuous further improvement, which is very important for us - there is always a better way

 

Quality Engineering we clearly understand incorporate concepts and quality techniques along all the productive system. Deploying organizational standards for control proceedings and auditing. With proper use of math and statistics to model manufacturing systems during production, we build a strong and efficient quality control model.
